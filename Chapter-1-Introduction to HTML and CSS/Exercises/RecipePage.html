Chicken Adobo

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made by simmering chicken in a savory, slightly tangy sauce of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic and all kinds of spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into different pieces)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
