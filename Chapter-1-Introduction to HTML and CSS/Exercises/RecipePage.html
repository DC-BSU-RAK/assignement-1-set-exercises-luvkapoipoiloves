Chicken Adobo

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made by simmering chicken in a savory, slightly tangy sauce of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic and all kinds of spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into different pieces)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, marinate the chicken with soy sauce and garlic for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the onions until soft.
  3. Add the marinated chicken (reserve the marinade) and brown on all sides.
  4. Pour in the reserved marinade, vinegar, water, bay leaves, and black peppercorns.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for 30-40 minutes until the chicken is tender.
  6. Optionally, add sugar to balance the flavors.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
